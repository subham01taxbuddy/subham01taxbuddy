Please follow following Steps to E verify your Income Tax Return

  • Step 1- Login to www.incometaxindiaefiling.gov.in
  • Step 2- click on “e-Verify Return” from the quick links
  • Step 3 - Enter PAN, Assessment year and acknowledgement number and click on continue.
  • Step 4 - Select any option to e-verify your return.
  • Step 5 - Enter OTP and click on Submit, Your return will get verified and transaction ID will get generated.
